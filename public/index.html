About

I have always found myself at the confluence of creativity and technology. From capturing musical expression in digital forms, to communicating technical solutions to non-technical people, I am happiest when I'm using both sides of my brain. The challenge of combining the logical reasoning of a program with the design of an attractive, intuitive interface is what makes web development really exciting for me.

Ever since my parents walked in the living room to find their little boy with a screwdriver - and our VCR in twenty pieces on the floor - I have always wanted to know how things work. I have a tendency to tinker until I fully understand a concept. This has served me well in troubleshooting, debugging, and building new things. Thankfully my parents didn’t scold, but fostered my curiosity and love of learning. And I *sort of* got the VCR back together.

I have been exploring web technologies for over a decade, and began studying programming in earnest in early 2015. I am seeking opportunities where I can write code full time, and projects that will grow my programming skills. Thank you for landing on my page and checking out what I’ve built.
